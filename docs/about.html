Got questions? We’ve got answers to the most common ones right here.

What types of services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services including web design, app development, branding, digital marketing, SEO, graphic design, and custom software solutions. Whether you're looking to create a new brand identity, develop a responsive website, or boost your online presence, we have you covered.

What do you need from me to start a project?

To get started, we need a bit of your time to understand your vision and objectives for the project. We’ll schedule a meeting to discuss your needs, goals, and preferences. Additionally, we require 10% of the total project budget upfront to begin the work.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline depends on the scope and complexity of the project. We always strive to deliver your project as soon as possible, ensuring there are no delays or loss of quality. After our initial meeting, we’ll provide a more accurate timeline tailored to your specific needs.

What is the cost of your services?

Our costs are based on the specifics of each project. We offer competitive pricing and a flexible payment plan, which allows us to deliver quality results at a lower cost than many competitors. We’ll discuss the budget in detail after understanding the scope of your project.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we offer free revisions on approved designs, with unlimited revisions until you’re satisfied. If we need to remake a design to meet your approval, we can provide up to 3-5 different variations depending on the project to ensure a smooth and efficient process.
